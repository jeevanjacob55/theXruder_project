Annual Filing & Compliance (After Incorporation)

Every year, your company must file several compliance documents. Here's what needs to be filed:

Document Details Cost Deadline
INC 20 A Filing for company commencement with government ₹4500 180 Days from Incorporation
KYC Know Your Customer for Directors and Shareholders ₹2500 per head September 30
ITR (Income Tax Return) Starting from ₹20k to ₹45k depending on the size ₹20,000 to ₹45,000 By September 30

Services You Need To Cover for Financial Year 2024-25:

  • Preparation & Filing of Form ADT-01 (Auditor Appointment)
  • Preparation of Balance Sheet, P&L Accounts, Audit Report, Director's Report, Extract of Annual Returns & Financial Statements
  • Preparation & Filing of Form AOC-4 (Financials Related Annual Return)
  • Preparation & Filing of Form MGT-7 (Management Related Annual Return)
  • Preparation of 04 Minutes of Board Meeting
  • Filing of Income Tax Returns (Company)

DSC Validity:

The Digital Signature Certificates (DSC) required for filings are valid for 2 years. Be sure to renew them for continuous compliance.

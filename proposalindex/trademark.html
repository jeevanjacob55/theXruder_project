  • The Name of the Brand / Slogan

  • The Date from which you have been using the brand name

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a trademark?

A trademark is a sign capable of distinguishing the goods or services of one enterprise from those of other enterprises. Trademarks are protected by intellectual property rights.

Why do I need to register my trademark?

Registering a trademark provides legal protection against infringement and gives you the exclusive right to use your brand name or logo in business, helping to prevent competitors from using similar marks.

How do I know if my trademark is available?

You can conduct a trademark search to check if your brand name or logo is already registered or in use. This can be done before applying for registration.
